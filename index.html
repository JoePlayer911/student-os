HOW TO PLAY

  • The game begins with seeds in each small hole.
  • Players take turns picking up all seeds from one hole and sowing them one-by-one into subsequent holes.
  • If your last seed lands in your Store (the large hole on your right), you get an extra turn.
  • If your last seed lands in an empty hole on your side, you capture that seed and all seeds in the opposite hole.
  • The game ends when all holes on one side are empty. The player with the most seeds wins.
